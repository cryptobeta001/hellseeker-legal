📄 Privacy Policy (Hell Seeker)

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

1. Overview

Hell Seeker is a fully offline game. This Privacy Policy explains that no personal data is collected, stored, or shared.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Device identifiers
  • Gameplay analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

3. Internet Usage

  • The game works completely offline
  • No internet connection is required to play
  • No background data is sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking systems are used

4. Third-Party Services

Hell Seeker does not use:

  • Ads networks
  • Analytics tools
  • Login systems
  • Cloud services

5. Children’s Privacy

Since no data is collected, the game is safe for all ages and does not process any children’s information.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the game is updated in the future to include online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
